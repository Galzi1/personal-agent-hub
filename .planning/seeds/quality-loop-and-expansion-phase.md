# Quality Loop & Near-Term Expansion

## Context
The research summary recommended 5 phases but the v1 roadmap correctly scoped to 4. This seed captures Phase 5 content so it's not lost when the team is ready to expand.

## What It Would Deliver
- Quality metrics and review loop (duplicate-collapse rate, freshness lag, user-marked hits, ignored items, missed major stories)
- Novelty / first-seen scoring relative to prior digest coverage
- Multi-day trend rollups across recurring signals
- Reusable workflow primitives for adjacent briefs (competitor monitoring, resource discovery)
- Optional downstream actions: "save as resource" or "turn into post idea"

## Pitfalls to Avoid
- Prompt thrash without metrics — measure before tuning
- Platform expansion before one workflow is proven
- Novelty scoring needs real data history to validate

## Research Needed
- Novelty scoring and trend detection algorithms once real digest data exists
- Quality scorecard definition: what makes a "good" digest measurable?
