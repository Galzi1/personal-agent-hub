# Rescope v1 Around One Visible Workflow

## Objective
Reduce early architecture risk by proving one end-to-end visible workflow before building the full worker set.

## Steps
1. Choose one high-value workflow to prove first
2. Limit v1 to the orchestrator plus one or two specialized workers
3. Define where handoffs must be visible in chat and where deeper detail belongs in the panel
4. Define approval and escalation rules for the chosen workflow
5. Run repeated trials and capture where the visibility model fails or creates trust
6. Expand the worker taxonomy only after the first workflow is stable and legible

## Success Criteria
- One workflow runs repeatedly with narrated handoffs
- A user can understand collaboration from chat alone at the high level
- The panel can explain why each major handoff happened
- Expansion criteria for the broader hub are based on real workflow evidence
